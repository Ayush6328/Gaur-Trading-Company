About Us

WELCOME TO Gaur Trading Company Gaur Trading Company is the one of the largest Wholesaler of Authentic Hand Rolled Premium Quality Papads, Bhujia & Namkeens, Sweets & Tea in Private Sector, in India, the Company owned business started at a primary level from home. Diversified with wholesale of Bikaneri Bhujia; a geographical delight that has highlighted the city of Bikaner on the map of India and a wide range of savoury Namkeens (Snacks), we also offer Bikaner speciality of Sweets and Assam Tea. We ensure the consistent quality of our products which are the key ingredients of Papad, Bhujia and Savoury Namkeens.
